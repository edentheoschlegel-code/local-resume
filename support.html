Common questions

Will my resume sync to my other devices?
No — by design. LocalResume stores everything only on the device you're using, with no account and no cloud sync. If you need your resume on another device, export it as a PDF and share it over (AirDrop, email, etc.).

I deleted the app / cleared my browser data — can I get my resume back?
Only from a backup you made yourself. Since nothing is ever uploaded, there's no copy on our end to restore from. If you saved a backup file (footer → Back up your data), you can import it with Restore from backup. This is the tradeoff for full privacy — see the Privacy Policy for details.

Is there a paid plan?
LocalResume is free to use. There's one optional upgrade — Pro, a one-time $12.99 purchase (no subscription) that unlocks Word (.docx) export, the cover letter builder, tailor-to-job hints, and unlimited saved versions. Payment is handled securely by Stripe and RevenueCat; you enter an email for your receipt only, it's not an account, and we never see your card. Your resume is never sent to them — see the Privacy Policy for the details.

I bought Pro — how do I keep it / move it to another browser?
Right after you buy, LocalResume shows a restore code (your "license card"). Because there are no accounts, that code is how you re-unlock Pro in another browser, so save it somewhere safe. Keep your Stripe receipt email too — it's your proof of purchase. The iPhone/iPad app sells Pro separately through the App Store. Lost your code? Email support@localresumeapp.com and we'll help.

I bought Pro — how do I keep it / move it to another device?
Your purchase belongs to your Apple Account. On another iPhone or iPad, open LocalResume and tap Restore Purchases — Pro unlocks again at no charge, no code needed. Your Apple receipt is your proof of purchase. Need a hand? Email support@localresumeapp.com.

Does LocalResume work offline?
Yes, once loaded. There's no server it depends on for day-to-day use.
